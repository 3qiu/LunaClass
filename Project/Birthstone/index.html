What are birthstones and what are their corresponding zodiac signs?

Birthstones carry secrets, attributes and lore that are unique to each gemstone. — Gemological Institute of America

A birthstone is a gemstone that represents the month or zodiac sign of when an individual was born. Historically, it is believed there that the twelve different stones originated from the Book of Exodus. High priests wore breastplates with the different stones implanted. In 1912, the official list was created by the Jewelers of America. Below find some quick facts about your birthstone and the zodiac sign that matches your birth month.
